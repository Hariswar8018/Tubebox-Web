Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Tubebox.

What is Tubebox?
Tubebox is a video sharing platform where users can upload, stream, and manage videos with unlimited storage.
Is Tubebox free to use?
Yes, Tubebox is completely free for users. Additional premium features may be introduced in the future.
How do I upload videos?
Simply log in to your account and use the upload button from your dashboard to add videos.
Is there a storage limit?
Currently, Tubebox offers unlimited storage for creators.
Can I download videos?
Downloading depends on the uploader's settings. Some videos may allow downloads while others may not.
How do I report copyright issues?
You can submit a DMCA request through our DMCA page or contact our support team directly.
Is my data secure?
Yes, we implement industry-standard security measures to protect user data and content.
Can I delete my account?
Yes, you can delete your account anytime from your account settings.
